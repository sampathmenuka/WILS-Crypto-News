The Cryptocurrency Industry

Introduction

Blockchain is the underlying technology behind cryptocurrencies. It is a type of distributed ledger that records transactions in a secure, transparent, and tamper-proof way.

How Blockchain Works

  • A block contains data (like a transaction), a timestamp, and a cryptographic hash.
  • Each block is linked to the previous one, forming a chain.
  • Once a block is added, the information in it cannot be changed, ensuring data integrity.
